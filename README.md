# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard Project
## 🎯 Project Objectives:
The primary goal of this project is to provide a comprehensive analysis of the organization's human capital. By leveraging data, we aim to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and provide actionable insights to management. 
**Key metrics analyzed:**
*   **Workforce Overview:** Current headcount, Average age, and Gender distribution.
*   **Retention & Turnover:** Monitoring turnover rates and termination reasons.
*   **Compensation Analysis:** Examining hourly pay rates across departments, positions, and states.
*   **Performance & Satisfaction:** Linking performance scores and manager effectiveness to employee satisfaction.
*   **Recruitment Strategy:** Analyzing the top recruitment sources and hiring trends by year.
*  ## 🛠️ Tools & Skills:
*   **Data Analysis:** Descriptive and Diagnostic analysis of HR metrics.
*   **Visualization:** Interactive Dashboards.
*   **Strategic Thinking:** Translating data into business recommendations.

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations:
Based on the findings, the following actions are recommended:
*   **Proactive Retention:** Conduct "Stay Interviews" to understand employee concerns before they decide to leave.
*   **Compensation Review:** Ensure high-performing employees are rewarded with competitive pay and bonuses.
*   **Leadership Development:** Provide targeted leadership training for managers associated with low team satisfaction scores.
*   **Future Talent Pipeline:** Focus on hiring younger talent and implementing succession planning for long-term stability.\
